# Notes on Gait

## Gait is not "the animal's joint angles"
### Gait i s the organized, repeating pattern by which the body and limbs produce locomotion
The useful abstraction is *gait = contact timing + foot placement + body posture + joint/force pattern*

## Gait has two main phases ##
## on the ground (stance) or in the air (swing)
for a leg: touchdown -> stance -> liftoff -> swing -> next touchdown
